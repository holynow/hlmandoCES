VISION

HL Mando is a leading global automotive parts supplier,
specialized in xEV and autonomous driving solutions with an A grade in the MSCI ESG ratings.
The company was founded in 1962 and is headquartered in South Korea.

HL Mando offers state-of-the-art mobility solutions including brake, steering, suspension, motors, and power electronics along with software solutions for mobility manufacturers and service providers.

HL Mando serves over 60 customers from over 50 locations worldwide, which encompasses local and global automakers in every major region of the world. HL Mando is pursuing a vision of creating a world where mobility can bring true freedom.

HL Klemove is a leading global autonomous driving solution provider with over 2,100 patents. The company started as a ADAS business unit of HL Mando, the global automotive chassis system supplier
then grew out of HL Mando to accelerate further as a global autonomous driving solution provider.

HL Klemove offers end-to-end solution for L2+/L3 autonomous driving and plans to launch next generation autonomous driving products such as advanced sensors and next generation architectures. The company serves over 50 customers. HL Klemove promises safe and comfortable driving through its advanced sensors and SW algorithm, on the strength of strong electronic product capabilities.
